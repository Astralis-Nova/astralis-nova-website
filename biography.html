Biography

Astralis Nova is the musical identity of Ramon Bivens, an independent Arizona artist and lifelong builder of worlds. His songs move through personal history, family, love, loss, imagination, and the vast quiet of the desert sky.

Rather than staying inside one musical lane, Astralis Nova follows the emotion of each story. Some songs look backward toward childhood and people who shaped his life. Others travel through science fiction, virtual worlds, military-family memories, romance, humor, and the strange beauty of simply being alive.

The project grew from Ramon's love of music, technology, gaming, storytelling, and the early internet. The Astralis Nova website carries that spirit forward as more than a streaming page. It is part music archive, part cosmic scrapbook, and part living gathering place where visitors can listen, leave messages, and add their own marks to the universe.

Astralis Nova is music for the memories we carry, the worlds we build, and the light we keep searching for.
